# Quick Guide, Sage Essence

How to use your booking system, made simple. No tech knowledge needed.

Your site: **thesageessence.com**

---

## In one sentence

Customers come to your page, request their clean, and pay on their own. You see
everything from a private panel.

---

## Your 2 addresses

- **Your page (what people see):** thesageessence.com
- **Your panel (just for you):** thesageessence.com/login

---

## How to sign in to your panel

1. Go to **thesageessence.com/login**
2. Type your password.
3. Done, you see everything.

To leave: **"Sign out"** button, top right.

> If you forget the password, message us and we reset it in minutes.

---

## What you see in the panel

Three lists, one below the other:

### 1. Bookings
All bookings, newest on top. For each one you see:
- What clean they asked for and when.
- How much it costs.
- Whether they paid or it is still pending.

### 2. Abandoned leads
People who started a booking and left their phone, but did not finish.
**This is money:** call or text them, they almost always close.

### 3. Customers
How many customers you have in total. The **"Export CSV"** button downloads the
list into an Excel sheet.

---

## What I do often

**See my bookings**
Open the panel, look at the "Bookings" list. At the top you can tap "confirmed" to
see only the ones already paid.

**Recover someone who did not finish**
Open the panel, look at "Abandoned leads", and reach out to them.

**Charge an extra on a booking**
Inside the booking there is a box: type what it is (e.g. "inside the fridge") and
the amount, then confirm. It charges the card they already left.

**Download the customer list**
In "Customers", tap "Export CSV".

---

## What happens on its own (you do nothing)

- **Payment:** the customer pays when booking, the money goes to your account.
- **Schedule:** the booking is added to your calendar by itself.
- **Emails:** the customer automatically gets the prep, reminder, and thank-you
  messages.

---

## If something is off

- **Cannot sign in:** check the password. If not, message us.
- **A booking says "Demo mode":** let us know, the database still needs to be
  turned on.
- **The customer did not get the email:** have them check spam. If it continues,
  let us know.
- **I want to change a price, text, or photo:** we do that, send us the details.

   up in Austin. Add the name, phone, area, and photos.

   helps Google find you faster.
